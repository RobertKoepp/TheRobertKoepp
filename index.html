About Me

For over five years, Robert Koepp has been a noteworthy leader in Warehouse Logistics. You may know him from his work as the Warehouse Logistics Manager at Mountain Comfort Furnishings, but Robert can also be credited with contributions to teaching children snowboarding in North Lake Tahoe Childrens Ski Schools, as well as actively participating in volunteer groups such as Clean Lake Tahoe. He has a strong passion for the outdoors, snowboarding, and music. Robert currently resides in North Lake Tahoe, California.
